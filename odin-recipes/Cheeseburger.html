Cheeseburger

  

Comfort Food

  

Cheeseburgers are a classic comfort food made with a grilled beef patty, melted cheese, and a bun, often topped with vegetables like lettuce, tomato, and condiments of your choice.

  

From a health perspective, cheeseburgers can offer real nutritional benefits when enjoyed in moderation. The beef patty provides high-quality protein...

How to Make

  
        
  1. Prepare the patties: Form ground beef into evenly sized patties and lightly season both sides with salt and pepper.
    2.     
  2. Heat the cooking surface: Preheat a grill or pan over medium-high heat and lightly oil it to prevent sticking.
    3.     
  3. Cook the patties: Place the patties on the grill or pan and cook for a few minutes per side until they reach your desired doneness.
    4.     
  4. Add the cheese: Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking and let it melt.
    5.     
  5. Assemble the burgers: Toast the buns if desired, then place the cheeseburgers on the buns and add toppings like lettuce, tomato, and condiments.
